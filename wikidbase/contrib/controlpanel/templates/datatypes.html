Relationships

Since wikidbase has no fixed data model, by default you can add any relationships you like among pages; however, sometimes you may want to tame this chaos by pre-defining the relationships of your page types, which is what you can do here. For example, if you have created two types of pages called 'Person' and 'Animal', you may want to create relationships between these such as 'Owner - Pet' or 'Vet - Animal', then you can write relational queries such as: show me all people where pets.name = 'Fido'; or show me people where pets.vets.pets.gender = male (which translates to a list of all people who have pets who have vets who also have pets of the male gender - phew!).
